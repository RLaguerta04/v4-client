Feature 1 · Today's Brief

Your AI daily stand-up.

Every morning the Brief reads the last 24 hours of coverage and tells you, in one glance, what changed and what deserves attention — before you've opened a single article.

  • Always-on summary. Headline call-out (“3 Tier 1 stories flagged”), a live freshness stamp, and momentum signals like “+22% mentions vs yesterday.”
  • Numbers up front. A persistent stat strip — mentions, combined AVE, sentiment split and Tier 1 count — visible without expanding anything.
  • Explains itself. A “Why this was picked” rationale on every spotlighted story, so the AI's choice is transparent and defensible.

Today's Brief · The three views

Read it your way.

Tab 1

Today's Spotlight

The single highest-impact story, fully unpacked: headline, publisher, total AVE, sentiment & velocity chips, the channels it spans, and the full list of related coverage — sortable, filterable by media type, and paginated.

Tab 2

Trending Now

A ranked leaderboard of story clusters by momentum. Each row shows tier, sentiment, article count and AVE — click any cluster to expand and explore every piece of coverage feeding it.

Tab 3

AI Analysis

One click generates a written synthesis of the day's coverage patterns — the narrative arc, what's driving it, and what to watch next — in plain, ready-to-share language.

Feature 2 · Activity Tracker

Where a story becomes a campaign.

The Tracker is the home for every initiative you're monitoring — press releases, events, crises, product launches and trending narratives — each with its own live coverage and reporting.

  • Fed by the Brief. Stories tracked from Today's Spotlight arrive pre-loaded and ready to scan.
  • Organised by type. Colour-coded activities (PR, Event, Crisis, Trend, Product) with search and filtering.
  • Living coverage. Each activity keeps its own article set, coverage snapshot and on-demand AI report.
